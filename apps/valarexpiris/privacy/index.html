Privacy Policy

Valar Expiris

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Valar Expiris is an API key lifecycle manager that runs entirely on your device. We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data.

Data Storage

  • API key values are stored exclusively in the macOS/iOS Keychain and optionally synced via iCloud Keychain — Apple's end-to-end encrypted credential sync service.
  • Metadata (service names, labels, tags, expiration dates, URLs) is stored locally using SwiftData and optionally synced via Apple's CloudKit.
  • All data remains under your control within Apple's ecosystem. The developer has no access to your data at any point.

Data Collection

We collect none of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data

Third-Party Services

Valar Expiris uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or external services. The app is built entirely with Apple frameworks.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your data may sync across your devices through Apple's infrastructure. This sync is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer cannot access any iCloud-synced data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
